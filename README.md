# Superstore Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard

![Dashboard](screenshots/dashboard_full.png)

## Project Overview
End-to-end sales analytics project analyzing 9,994 US retail orders across 4 years (2014–2017) using Microsoft Excel. Built an interactive dashboard with business findings and actionable recommendations.

**Dataset:** [Kaggle — Superstore Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final)  
**Tool:** Microsoft Excel  
**Analyst:** Jyoti Gupta

---

## Business Problem
Identify which products, categories and regions are driving profit — and which are causing losses — to support data-backed business decisions.

---

## Key Findings

| # | Finding | Data |
|---|---------|------|
| 1 | Technology has highest profit margin | 17.4% margin |
| 2 | Furniture critically underperforms | Only 2.5% margin |
| 3 | Three sub-categories losing money | -$22,386 combined |
| 4 | West region leads all regions | $725K sales, $108K profit |
| 5 | Q4 drives 40% of annual revenue | November is peak month |

---

## Dashboard Features
- 4 KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin %, Total Orders
- 4 Charts: Monthly Trend, Category Sales, Sub-Category Profit, Regional Sales
- 3 Interactive Slicers: Year, Region, Category
- Professional dark BI theme

## Business Recommendations
- Cap Furniture discounts at 15% immediately
- Prioritize Technology investment — highest ROI category
- Discontinue Tables if losses persist after 2 quarters
- Replicate West region strategy in South region
- Increase Q4 inventory planning from August onwards
